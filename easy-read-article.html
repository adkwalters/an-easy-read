More than 100,000 people watched as Hila and Ethan Klein learned that Hila was pregnant with their child, in an event streamed live on the Internet. People commented that Hila was a "queen" and that they were "crying of happiness".

Hila and Ethan are very popular. They make videos on Youtube. Some of their videos are about them doing funny things. Some of their videos show them watching other people’s videos. These days, they make videos where they talk to other people about interesting things. Many people like these videos a lot. Some of these people like Hila and Ethan’s videos so much that they feel like real friends.

Over the past decade, Hila and Ethan Klein have become Internet celebrities for their popular YouTube channel. They started off making videos of themselves doing funny things or reacting to other people’s videos. Next, they started making videos where they talk to other people about subjects that are important and popular. As a result, they have gained millions of fans, some of whom feel very close to the couple, almost as though they know Hila and Ethan personally.

Hila and Ethan talk a lot about things that happen to them. They have a baby and want to have another baby. However, it isn’t easy for them. They need a doctor to help them. In Hila and Ethan’s videos, they talk about wanting another baby. They talk about going to the doctor and what the doctors say. When they find out that they are going to have a baby, all their fans find out at the same time. This is very unusual because people normally only tell their best friends and family.

Hila and Ethan share many parts of their personal lives with their online audience. They share their struggles to have a second child, and explain the medical steps they take to become pregnant. Their videos include information and advice they get from doctors. Their fans are treated like the closest of friends and family when Hila discovers that she is pregnant live on their channel.
